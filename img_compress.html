How to Compress Images Online

Our free image compression tool makes it easy to reduce file size while maintaining visual quality. Follow these simple steps:

  1. Upload your image by dragging and dropping or clicking to browse
  2. Adjust the compression level using the slider
  3. Select your desired output format (JPG, PNG, or WebP)
  4. Click "Compress Image" to process your file
  5. Download your optimized image

Why Compress Images?

Image compression is essential for:

  • Faster website loading times
  • Reduced bandwidth usage
  • Smaller storage requirements
  • Improved SEO rankings (Google considers page speed)
  • Better user experience on mobile devices
